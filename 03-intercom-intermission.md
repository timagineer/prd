## Rules

> - DO NOT add the solution here. Solution design happens separately (typically in Figma).
> - Always use plain simple English, no technical terminology or codenames.
> - An Intermission MUST fit on a printed A4 page. If it does not, you haven't a clear enough view of the problem yet.
