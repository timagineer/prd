## How To Use This Template

1. **Crystallize the problem** — Fill out the Problem Statement section first. If you can't explain it clearly, you don't understand it well enough yet.
2. **Align with team and stakeholders** — Share the draft. Get feedback. Integrate. Re-share.
3. **Keep coming back to the problem** — In every design review, every progress update, ask: "Are we still solving the original problem?"
