## How To Use This Template

1. **Living document** — Update continuously as designs evolve. Embed Figma files so they auto-update.
2. **Interactive milestones** — Track progress directly in the doc, not in a separate PM tool.
3. **Launch checklist** — Check items off as you go. The PRD becomes your launch tracker.
4. **Design as source of truth** — Embedded Figma files mean the design IS the spec, always current.
