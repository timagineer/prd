## How To Use This Template

1. **Don't skip stages** — The mandatory contributor reviews at each stage create accountability without waterfall.
2. **Problem before solution** — Complete Part 1 and get sign-off BEFORE writing Part 2.
3. **Draw the perimeter** — Part 2 defines boundaries. The team fills in how to build it.
4. **Living document** — Update the Change Log as decisions evolve.
