# Amazon Working Backwards PR/FAQ

> Inverts traditional product development. Write a press release announcing the product as if it's already launched — forcing clarity on customer value before building begins.
> "Once we have gone through the process of creating the press release, FAQ, mockups, and user manuals, it is amazing how much clearer it is what you are planning to build." — Werner Vogels, CTO, Amazon
> Best for: Major new products, strategic initiatives, companies comfortable with writing-heavy culture.

## How To Use This Template

1. **Write the press release FIRST** — Before any design or engineering work. If you can't write a compelling press release, the product idea needs more work.
2. **Iterate heavily** — Most PR/FAQs get rejected. That's a feature (cheap idea validation), not a bug.
3. **Customer obsession** — Every sentence in the press release should be from the customer's perspective. No internal jargon.
4. **Treat rejection as success** — A rejected PR/FAQ saved you months of building the wrong thing.
