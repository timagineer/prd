## How To Use This Template

1. **Shape before pitching** — Do the shaping work (find the elements, de-risk the concept) before writing this up.
2. **Post asynchronously** — Let stakeholders read on their own time. Keep the betting table short.
3. **Bet, don't plan** — This pitch is a potential bet, not a guaranteed project. It may get picked up or shelved.
4. **Preserve builder autonomy** — The solution section draws the boundaries. The team fills in the details.
